Focaccia with caramelized onions cramberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appettizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favourite holiday starter!

Image: Sally Vegas

FEATURE IN:

Fresh cramberries recipes that go beyond sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra vergin oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic oil
  • 1 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces bire, cut into cubes (scrapes rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed.
